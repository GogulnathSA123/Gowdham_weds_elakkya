The Prelude

In the quiet space where dreams collide,

Two hearts found home, side by side.

A canvas painted with stardust and grace,

Now finds its anchor in love's embrace.

Elakkya—a whisper of the morning breeze,

A song of light that puts the mind at ease.

With eyes reflecting the horizon's gleam,

She walks as the keeper of a beautiful dream.

Gowdham Raj—the silent, steady shore,

A gentle strength to lean on evermore.

With steps of purpose and a heart so true,

His path was drawn to wind its way to you.

In Ponamaravathi, where the old winds blow,

And fields of golden warmth softly glow,

On the thirteenth of September, the stars shall align,

To seal a promise, eternal, divine.

Join us as they begin their greatest journey together.

A Union of Souls

The Harmony of E & G

Love is not just looking at one another, it is looking outward together in the same direction. Elakkya and Gowdham Raj bring two worlds together—where creativity meets stability, and poetry meets purpose.

Their union is a celebration of family, culture, and friendship. From the peaceful town of Ponamaravathi to the journey that lies ahead, they step forward with gratitude, holding the blessings of their elders and the laughter of their friends.

"Like notes of a song in perfect key, they make a beautiful symphony."
